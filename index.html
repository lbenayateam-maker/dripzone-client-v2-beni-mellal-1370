Frequently Investigated Policies

Is there a dress code specification in place?

We enforce a strict smart-casual dress code policy. Sports items, athletic wear, and casual sandals are restricted inside the main dining salons after 6:00 PM.

Do you offer comprehensive vegetarian options?

Yes, our traditional Moroccan kitchen prepares legendary non-meat courses, including the layered Couscous with Seven Vegetables and custom fire-roasted eggplant Zaalouk purees.

Can I host complete private venue buyouts?

Absolutely. Our riad palace architecture can support up to 180 guests for private banquets, corporate configurations, or custom events with curated entertainment rosters.

Is valet parking support accessible at the gate?

Yes, secure personal vehicle valet assistance is stationed right outside our drop-off driveway point throughout our evening operating frames.
